About us

Welcome to Yoko's Kitchen, a Japanese cooking school located in the heart of Kivimäe, Tallinn. Our school is named after our head chef, Yoko, who has over 20 years of experience in Japanese cuisine. At Yoko's Kitchen, we offer hands-on cooking classes that focus on traditional Japanese dishes such as sushi, ramen, and tempura, as well as unique creations that showcase Yoko's creativity and passion for food. Our classes are designed to provide a fun and engaging atmosphere where students of all skill levels can learn new cooking techniques and discover the art of Japanese cuisine. Join us at 27 Pargi Street, Kivimäe, Tallinn and let's cook together!
