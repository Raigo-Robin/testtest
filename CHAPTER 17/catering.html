Catering

At Yoko's Kitchen, where we offer authentic Japanese catering services for all your special events. Our catering menu features a variety of traditional Japanese dishes such as sushi, ramen, tempura, and bento boxes. We use only the freshest ingredients and traditional cooking techniques to ensure that every dish is of the highest quality and bursting with flavor.
Our catering menu offers a range of options to fit any occasion and budget. Whether you are planning a small gathering or a large corporate event, we have you covered. Our catering packages include:

  • Sushi Platters: A variety of sushi rolls made with the freshest ingredients.
  • Ramen Bar: Create your own ramen bowls with your choice of broth, toppings, and noodles.
  • Bento Boxes: A traditional Japanese lunch box filled with rice, protein, and vegetables.
  • Tempura Platters: An assortment of lightly battered and fried vegetables and proteins.
  • Combination Packages: Choose a combination of our most popular dishes to create the perfect meal.

Our catering prices vary based on the package and number of guests, but we strive to offer competitive and affordable rates for all our customers. Contact us today to discuss your catering needs and let us bring the delicious flavors of Japan to your event.
